This is the next in a series of Pierson Pioneer Teas in honor of the college’s alumnae, and the special guests are two alumnae from the Class of 1977. Weili Cheng is a Pierson Fellow and Executive Director of the Yale Alumni Association (YAA). She holds a law degree from Georgetown University and previously worked as legal counsel for major hotel corporations. Anne Fraser Keating holds an MBA from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania and is the founder of Fraser Keating Associates, LLC. An accomplished three sport athlete at Yale, she earned nine varsity letters in field hockey, basketball and lacrosse, and later was a member of the U.S. Field Hockey and Lacrosse Teams.

This Tea will be moderated by two members of the Pierson Women at 50 Committee, Courtney Green (PC '21) and Hailey Andrews (PC '22).
