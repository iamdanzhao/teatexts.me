Details

J.R. Martinez was born in Shreveport, Louisiana to his mom, Maria Zavala, an immigrant from El Salvador. In 2002 he joined the army and deployed to Iraq in 2003 where, during a routine patrol, his Humvee hit a roadside bomb, leaving J.R. Martinez covered in severe burns. He survived, and has gone on to become an actor, motivational speaker, New York Times best-selling author, and Dancing with the Stars season 13 winner. He has spoken to troops across the world, as well as serving as the keynote speaker for Delta, Wells Fargo, Verizon, State Farm, General Motors, Red Cross and numerous other Fortune 500 companies, non-profits and universities. He will be speaking at Silliman’s College Tea on Thursday 11/14 at 4pm.
