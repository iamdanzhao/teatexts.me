TeaTexts

TeaTexts is a new way to stay informed of the latest residential college teas at Yale College. All event are free to Yale students, and they're a great way to gear from innovators, academics, artists, musicians, and writers in a variety of subject areas.

TeaTexts is currently in pilot phase. Contact daniel.zhao@yale.edu with any questions or comments, or if there is a college tea you would like to promote.
