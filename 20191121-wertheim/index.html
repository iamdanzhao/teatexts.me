L. Jon Wertheim is editor-in-chief of Sports Illustrated, where he has been a senior writer since 1999. He is a contributing correspondent for 60 Minutes, and has also appeared on the sports edition of 60 Minutes as a commentator and reporter.

His work has been anthologized in The Best American Sports Writing series four times, as well as in The Best American Crime Writing series, and he is the author of nine books, including New York Times bestsellers Scorecasting: The Hidden Influences Behind How Sports Are Played and Games are Won and You Can’t Make this Up. Wertheim also serves as a commentator for The Tennis Channel during its coverage of the four annual Grand Slam tournaments. A native of Bloomington, Ind., Wertheim graduated from Yale College in 1993 and from the University of Pennsylvania Law School in 1997. He resides in New York City.
