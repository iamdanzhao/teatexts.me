Details

P.J. Vogt co-hosts the podcast Reply All along with Alex Goldman. Reply All features stories about how people shape the internet, and how the internet shapes people. It premiered November 24, 2014, and has an average of 5 stars on Apple Podcasts, with over 19,000 reviews. It has been nominated for numerous rewards, including a 2016 Webby Nominee for Best Podcast and a 2016 MORA nominee for "Best Online Talk Show" in education. He will be speaking at Silliman’s College Tea on Monday 11/18 at 4pm.
